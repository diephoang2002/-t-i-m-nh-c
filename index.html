QUICK START GUIDE
It's easy to get started!
  • 1KNOW YOUR PD
    We need your PD (pupillary distance) to make your glasses. If you don’t have it already, check out our DIY How to Measure Your PD video.
  • 2VIRTUAL TRY-ON
    See exactly how any frame will look and fit on your face with our 180-degree Virtual Try-On tool.
  • 3FIND A PAIR YOU'LL LOVE
    Browse our Men, Women, Kids, or Featured pages and use our handy filters to narrow down your search. All glasses include FREE anti-scratch coating, single vision lens, UV protection, hard case, and cleaning cloth.
  • 4HAVE YOUR PRESCRIPTION READY
    You'll need to type in your prescription to complete your order, so have it handy. Need help reading your prescription? Check our FAQ.
